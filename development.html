Gimbal Operator | Focus Puller

Working in the industry since 2017 when I graduated from Lusófona University. Started my career as a 2º assistant camera working on all kind of sets from commercials to features.

Have been a focus puller since 2020 enjoying every moment of it but my biggest love has always been camera operating. With that in mind and after doing some jobs with rented gimbals, I finally bought my Movi Pro and since then I’m living the dream.

If you are reading this maybe it’s because you have a challenge for me and thinking if I’m the one. Bring it on ! Together we will make it happen.
